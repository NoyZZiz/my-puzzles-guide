TRAP WORKSHOP GUIDE

Master castle defense with the right traps. Covers all trap types, levels, capacities, and counter-strategies.

Overview

The Trap Workshop unlocks at Castle Level 4 and is your primary defense against raids and rallies. After Level 10, you lose protection without Shield of Grace - making traps essential.

WARNING: No safety post-Castle Level 10 without Shield of Grace. Stock your traps!

Defense Strategy

01.

Stock anti-predator traps based on your server's meta. If rallies are cavalry-heavy, max Boulder production.

02.

Arrow Towers are essential against ranged attackers - the most common rally composition.

03.

Combine traps with formations. "Rally trap" setups use ranged formations to eat mixed rallies.

04.

Max capacity via upgrades. Higher Workshop level = more traps deployed simultaneously.

05.

Always keep Shield of Grace active if you're below Castle 15 or can't defend a full rally.
