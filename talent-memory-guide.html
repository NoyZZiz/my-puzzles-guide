Talent Memory Guide

Master your talents for Level 10 VIP and beyond

Written by: Lisette

Introduction

Congratulations on reaching Level 10 VIP and unlocking the free talent memory reset!

Now that you're at VIP 10, it's essential to understand how to optimize the Talent Memory for the best results. You can only have three talent sets until you reach VIP 11. After reaching VIP 11, you can unlock the ability to have four talent sets, which gives you even more flexibility to customize your strategy.

Talent Sets Overview

There are three main Talent Sets to focus on at VIP 10:

  • Balance Set
  • Attack Set
  • Production Set

Balance Set

The Balance Set provides buffs for:

  • Training (capacity and speed)
  • Healing (capacity and speed)
  • Wall defense, storage capacity, troop load
  • Stamina replenishment and march speed

Special Talents:

  • Hunt Master: Reduces stamina cost by 20% for 10 minutes (with a 24-hour cooldown).
  • Cost Reduction: Reduces the cost of training and healing troops by 25% for 60 minutes (with a 24-hour cooldown).
  • Instant Heal: It instantly restores 10% of the wounded troops in your infirmary at no cost. (24-hour cooldown)

Attack Set

The Attack Set enhances:

  • Troop strength (attack, defense, health)
  • Troop size and march speed
  • Reduces troop losses after battle

Special Talents:

  • Recall: Instantly recalls all troops in 3 seconds (with an 8-hour cooldown).
  • Mega March: Increases max troop size by 10% for 30 minutes (with a 12-hour cooldown).
  • Divine Blessing: Within 30 minutes of activation, 30% of the troops that are killed will be turned into wounded troops, up to the infirmary cap (24-hour cooldown)

Production Set

The Production Set focuses on improving:

  • Building and research times
  • Resource production and gathering speed

Special Talents:

  • Fast Build: Reduces building time for constructions started within 5 minutes (with a 24-hour cooldown).
  • Swift Gatherer: Instantly grants the maximum amount of resources for your gathering troops (excluding alliance and diamond mines) with a 24-hour cooldown.
  • Fast Producer: Instantly produce 5hrs of resources from your resources building (12-hour cooldown)

Talent Set Strategy

With three preset talent sets at VIP 10 and the ability to have four sets at VIP 11, you have a great deal of flexibility. You are free to change your set anytime based on your needs:

  • If you need more attack power or are preparing for rallies, switch to the Attack Set.
  • If you want faster gathering of resources, or need to boost training and building speeds, the Balance Set or Production Set is your go-to.
  • It's highly recommended to switch your sets frequently, depending on the situation, as doing so will help you adapt quickly to your evolving needs.

Example Talent Allocation

For example, if you're Level 51 with 258 talent points, you might allocate them as follows:

  • Attack Set: For enhancing troop power and damage.
  • Balance Set: For training, healing, and stamina replenishment.
  • Production Set: For faster building and resource gathering.

By constantly adapting and switching between these sets, you can optimize your growth and strategy, depending on your in-game needs.
